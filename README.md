# Overview
Expanded Weaponry is a mod that enhances Minecraft combat in many ways. Its main focus is on providing more variety and choice in equipment.


# New items
<details>
<summary>Melee weapons</summary>

### Hammer
![Crafting recipe of the hammer](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/f6d2bb0a7705770d93ff0054c7b06ac57c8d5c86.png)

Heavy weapon capable of killing enemies with just 2 blows. Available in all materials.
- very high damage
- very slow attack speed
- two-handed

### Dagger
![Crafting recipe of the dagger](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/edd5be07a573a413261d55ba94e30e95262800f1.png)

Lightweight and fast weapon that works the best at fighting multiple weaker opponents. Available in all materials.
- low damage
- fast attack speed
- low range
