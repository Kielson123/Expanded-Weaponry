# Overview
Expanded Weaponry is a mod that enhances Minecraft combat in many ways. Its main focus is on providing more variety and choice in equipment.

I highly advise combining this mod with [Better Combat](https://modrinth.com/mod/better-combat) as it offers cool animations that enhance gameplay and enable you to dual-wield weapons.

### Dagger
![Crafting recipe of the dagger](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/549742128954aaa1c02c0f6da65d06ab6121727f.png)

Lightweight and fast weapon that works the best at fighting multiple weaker opponents. Available in all materials.

- very fast attack speed
- low range

![Crafting recipe of the twin blade sword](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/c8684f4f5703f5f7e5affcb9d290522c441e3bf2.png)

Fast and stylish weapon meant for fighting swarms of enemies. Available in all materials.
- medium damage
- fast attack speed
- two-handed

### Greatsword
![Crafting recipe of the greatsword](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/af7aba7659805d307bd83d96a93b9062ddb9434d.png)
